Multiblock Changes
Be careful what you are building!

Now, there are changes in creating multiblock structures. To create Arc Furnace you will need Fire Bricks and the Steel Plated Block from TerraFirmaCraft instead of IE Blast Bricks and a separately crafted IE steel storage block. Also, keep in mind that you don't need Blast Furnace from Immersive Engineering since you have it in TerraFirmaCraft! Additionally, you don't need Alloy Kiln because, you know, you can make alloys in TerraFirmaCraft.<br>
