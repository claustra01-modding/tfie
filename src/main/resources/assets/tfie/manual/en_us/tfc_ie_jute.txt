Industrial Hemp is a remarkable plant, but today we are going to talk about Jute! Not only are its seeds useful for the creation of <link;industrial_fluids;Biodiesel;biodiesel>, Jute Fiber is also used for the creation of Burlap Cloth, which is an alternative of <link;industrial_hemp;Tough Fabric;fabric_recipe>.<br>
The seeds can be obtained by harvesting Wild Jute.<np>
Similar to Tough Fabric, Burlap Cloth is a resilient weave made from Jute Fiber. It's used to create <link;kinetic_generators;Improved Windmills;sail_recipe>, <link;balloon;Balloons;start> as well as <link;industrial_hemp;Jump Cushions;cushion_recipe> and <link;strip_curtains;Strip Curtains>.
